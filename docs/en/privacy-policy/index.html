Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with our services, we may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity information: name, company name, username, identification information and other details required to establish or manage a business relationship.
  • Contact information: email address, postal address, telephone number and other communication details.
  • Contract and business information: project requirements, communications, agreements, non-disclosure agreements (NDAs), statements of work and other information necessary to provide freelance services.
  • Financial and fiscal information: billing information, invoices, payment records, tax identification information and information required for accounting and legal compliance.
  • Technical information: IP addresses, browser information, operating system information, device information, access logs and security-related information.
  • Security and compliance information: information necessary to prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, money laundering, sanctions violations or other unlawful activity.

Why we collect information

We process personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing, maintaining and improving our services.
  • Communicating with clients and responding to requests.
  • Preparing, negotiating and fulfilling contracts and agreements.
  • Managing invoices, payments, accounting obligations and tax compliance.
  • Protecting our services against fraud, abuse, unauthorized access and security threats.
  • Performing legal and regulatory obligations, including applicable sanctions and compliance requirements.

Legal basis for processing

Under the GDPR, we process personal information based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual necessity: processing information required to provide services, negotiate agreements and fulfill contractual obligations.
  • Legal obligations: processing required for accounting, taxation, regulatory and compliance purposes.
  • Legitimate interests: protecting our services, preventing abuse, improving reliability and ensuring security.
  • Consent: where consent is required for optional processing activities.

Non-disclosure agreements and confidential information

During freelance engagements, we may process confidential information provided by clients under contractual obligations, including NDAs. Such information is handled only for the purposes agreed upon with the relevant parties and is protected according to applicable contractual and legal requirements.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only where necessary, including with:

  • Service providers that support our operations, such as hosting, payment processing, accounting or communication providers.
  • Professional advisors, where required for legal, financial or compliance purposes.
  • Authorities or institutions where disclosure is required by law or necessary to comply with legal obligations.

International transfers

If personal information is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we take appropriate measures to ensure that the transfer complies with applicable data protection requirements.

Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

Certain information may be retained for longer periods where required by law, including fiscal, accounting, contractual or compliance obligations.

Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, loss or destruction.

Your rights under GDPR

Depending on applicable law, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal information.
  • Right to correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to request deletion of your information.
  • Right to restrict or object to certain processing activities.
  • Right to request portability of your information.
  • Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

You may exercise these rights by contacting us through the contact information provided on this website.

Right to lodge a complaint

If you believe your rights have been violated, you may contact the relevant data protection supervisory authority in your jurisdiction.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements or processing activities. The latest version will always be published on this page.

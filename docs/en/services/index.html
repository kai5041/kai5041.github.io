    How it works

    Pricing Classification

    Pricing may vary depending on the Client’s tax jurisdiction and applicable classification. Clients may provide valid tax residency or fiscal documentation for classification purposes. Where such documentation is provided, the Operator may apply region-specific pricing as defined in the relevant offer.

    1. The Client contacts Operator Kai stating their needs.

    2. The Operator prepares and provides a minimum viable proposal for the Client.

    3. Once agreed, the Operator sends the Contract for the Client to sign (wet or digital signatures accepted) which regulates the relationship and applicable terms, including pricing classification.

    4. The Operator sends the Project Blueprint for the Client to evaluate. If approved, the Client must sign it. The Project Blueprint defines Scope, Milestones, Installments, and Operational Notes.

    5. Pricing and scope are defined in the Project Blueprint. The Project Blueprint establishes the initial scope, milestones, and total cost. Any changes to scope after approval require a revised or extended Project Blueprint, which may adjust pricing accordingly.

    6. The commission is paid in installments defined by the Project Blueprint.

    7. The Operator provides proof of completion of each milestone.

    8. The Client pays the final remaining amount, and the Operator delivers the project.

    Standard international pricing applies by default. Clients incorporated in Japan are eligible for Japan localized pricing billed in JPY and independent of foreign exchange rates.

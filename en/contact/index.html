How the process works

You will be guided through the entire process from initial contact to final delivery. Each step is handled together with clear instructions and support.


Step 1 - Initial contact

You send a general description of your request to one of the contact methods listed below.


Step 2 - Project review and compliance check

The request is evaluated for feasibility and scope alignment.


The following information is required before acceptance:

  • Billing name (full name or legal entity name, as it will appear on the invoice)
  • Country of residence and Country of Incorporation (if legal entity)
  • Tax identification details if applicable
  • Billing address and email address for invoice delivery

Compliance screening is mandatory. Services cannot be provided without verification of applicable legal and tax requirements.

Step 3 - Confirmation and Agreement

Scope, pricing, and timeline are confirmed. Agreements may be signed using either a recognized electronic signature (eIDAS-compliant) or a scanned handwritten signature in PDF.


Tax Residency Certificate (Attestato di Residenza Fiscale) is provided at agreement stage to determine applicable withholding tax treatment.

Step 4 - Development Phase

Work begins after confirmation and, where applicable, after receipt of the agreed upfront payment. Progress updates and previews are provided when applicable.

Source code is not included in the previews.


Step 5 - Delivery

Final product is delivered after payment confirmation.


Step 6 - Setup and Handover

System setup assistance is provided if included in the agreement.


Step 7 - Post-delivery support

48h support window after setup completion.


After this, the service is considered completed.
